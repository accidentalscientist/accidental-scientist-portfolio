National Electricity Market: Fuel Mix Generation

The National Electricity Market (NEM) is the interconnected grid spanning Australia's eastern and southern states: New South Wales, Queensland, Victoria, South Australia and Tasmania. This dashboard tracks how that electricity is generated, by fuel source and region. The headline breakdown sums the most recent 7 days of generation, while the chart further down follows the renewables-versus-fossil-fuels balance across the last three months.
