{# ── Who I am ── #}

About

Data, Systems, and Human Performance.

My work and my curiosity sit at the meeting point of the energy economy, the systems that shape it, and how people adapt, perform, and change. I came to this work sideways — which is why I call myself the Accidental Scientist.

{# ── Interests ── #}
01

Energy Systems

The future of the energy economy, the data behind it, and the climate policy steering the shift to renewables.

02

Data Stories

A genuine love of finding the story in the numbers — time-series, visualisation, and building tools that make data legible to others.

03

Human Performance

Football analytics, running analytics, and how the body adapts to stress over time — plus a deep interest in behaviour change and habit formation.

04

Commercial Intelligence

Reading commercial risk before it becomes a lost renewal — portfolio health, revenue retention, and the dashboards that turn messy account data into a decision a team can actually act on.
