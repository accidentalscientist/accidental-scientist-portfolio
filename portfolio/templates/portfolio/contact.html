About

Thibault Aymonier-Newman

I'm a data analyst and storyteller based in Sydney, working at the intersection of energy systems, data science, and human performance. My work focuses on making complex systems legible — whether that's the National Electricity Market, climate data, or how a runner's body responds to training load over time. I came to this work sideways, which is why I call myself the Accidental Scientist.

{# ── Interests ── #}
⚡

Energy transition

📊

Data science & methods

Time-series analysis, visualisation, Python, Django, and building tools that make data accessible to non-specialists.

🏃

Sport & performance

Parkrun, running analytics, and what sport science can teach us about how humans adapt to stress over time.

🌏

Society & policy

Climate policy, international development, and the social systems that sit beneath the energy transition.
